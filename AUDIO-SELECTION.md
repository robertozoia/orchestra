## Selection Rationale

### Why Fortissimo (Loud)?
- Maximum clarity for learning
- Easy to hear distinct characteristics
- Better for distinguishing similar instruments

### Why A Notes (Where Available)?
- Consistent pitch reference across instruments
- Natural tuning note for orchestra (A440)
- Available across most instrument ranges

### Why 1-Second Duration?
- Long enough to hear timbre
- Short enough for quick rounds
- Good balance for web loading
