## Sample Types Explained

### Crescendo
Starts soft and gradually gets louder - very dramatic and easy to identify

### Cresc-Decresc (Crescendo-Decrescendo)
Starts soft, gets loud, then gets soft again - shows full dynamic range

### Sustained Fortissimo
Continuous loud note - excellent for hearing timbre clearly

### Roll
Rapid repeated notes (percussion) - characteristic sound for drums/mallets
